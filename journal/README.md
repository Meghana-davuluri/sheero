# journal — Decision and Session History

This folder is the system's long-term memory. It captures what decisions were made, why they were made, and what happened in each session. Over time it becomes a searchable record of your thinking and progress.

---

## Files in This Folder

| File               | Purpose                                                                     |
|--------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| `decisions_log.md` | Major decisions — date, context, reasoning, and outcome                     |
| `session_log.md`   | Session summaries — what was worked on, what changed, what to carry forward |

---

## How to Use This Folder

- RIGGS appends to these files automatically during check-ins and sessions
- Do not delete entries — the log is most valuable when it's complete
- Review `decisions_log.md` before making major decisions — you may have already reasoned through something similar
- `session_log.md` is how RIGGS maintains continuity across conversations that don't share context
