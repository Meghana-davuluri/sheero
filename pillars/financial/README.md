# pillars/financial — Finances

This pillar covers your financial life — budget, debt, income, savings, and financial goals.

---

## What Belongs Here

- Budget and expense tracking
- Debt inventory and payoff strategy
- Income logs (salary, freelance, side income)
- Savings goals and progress
- Financial decisions and reasoning
- Accounts and institutions overview

---

## Example Files

- `budget.md` — monthly budget framework
- `debt_status.md` — debt breakdown, balances, and payoff strategy
- `income_log.md` — monthly income tracking
- `financial_goals.md` — short and long-term financial targets

---

## How Sheero Uses This Folder

Sheero references this when you ask about financial decisions, income strategy, or debt management. It will not track spending automatically — you bring the data, Sheero helps you interpret and act on it.
