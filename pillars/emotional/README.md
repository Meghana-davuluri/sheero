# pillars/emotional — Emotional Health

This pillar covers your inner life — habits, routines, emotional patterns, journaling frameworks, and creative or expressive projects.

---

## What Belongs Here

- Daily habit and routine frameworks
- Journaling prompts or reflection systems
- Notes from therapy, coaching, or inner work
- Creative projects with emotional significance (writing, music, art)
- Patterns you're tracking or trying to rewire

---

## Example Files

- `daily_routine.md` — your current morning/evening routine structure
- `habit_stack.md` — habits you're building and their triggers
- `journaling_prompts.md` — prompts for morning or evening reflection

---

## How RIGGS Uses This Folder

RIGGS references this during check-ins when emotional context is relevant. It can suggest prompts, flag patterns in your journal entries, and help you design or adjust routines.
