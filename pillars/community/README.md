# pillars/community — Community and Public Presence

This pillar covers your participation in communities and your public presence — online platforms, social media strategy, community projects, and any work that extends outward beyond your personal life.

---

## What Belongs Here

- Social media strategy and content plans
- Community projects you're involved in or building
- Public writing, newsletters, or content pipelines
- Platform profiles and account notes
- Brand and messaging frameworks

---

## Example Files

- `social_media.md` — platforms, handles, strategy, and posting cadence
- `x_content_plan.md` — content pillars, thread ideas, build-in-public log
- `newsletter.md` — audience, format, publishing schedule
- `community_projects.md` — active communities you're building or participating in

---

## How RIGGS Uses This Folder

RIGGS references this when you ask about content creation, platform strategy, community building, or public-facing projects. It can help draft posts, plan content calendars, and track publishing commitments.
