# pillars/spiritual — Philosophy and Values

This pillar holds your philosophical and spiritual framework — the principles you operate by, the texts that shaped your thinking, and the values you're trying to live.

---

## What Belongs Here

- Core values and guiding principles
- Philosophical frameworks you study or practice (Stoicism, etc.)
- Sacred or significant texts (full text or excerpts)
- Personal precepts, credos, or manifestos
- Notes from philosophical reading or reflection

---

## Example Files

- `core_values.md` — your named values with explanations
- `precepts.md` — daily or situational principles
- `reading_list.md` — philosophical texts you're working through
- `stoicism/` — subfolder for Stoic practice (Meditations, Enchiridion, etc.)

---

## How RIGGS Uses This Folder

RIGGS can surface a relevant precept during a morning check-in, reference your values when advising on a decision, or pull a passage from a text that matches your current situation. The more you put here, the more grounded the assistance becomes.
