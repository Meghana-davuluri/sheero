# pillars/environmental — Environment and Space

This pillar covers your physical environment — where you live, where you work, property and land projects, and any plans related to your physical space.

---

## What Belongs Here

- Housing plans, lease info, or property details
- Location research (considering a move, evaluating areas)
- Home improvement or land projects
- Homestead or self-sufficiency planning
- Environment-related goals and timelines

---

## Example Files

- `housing.md` — current living situation, lease terms, plans
- `location_research.md` — notes on places you're considering
- `homestead_plan.md` — land, infrastructure, and self-sufficiency goals
- `property_projects.md` — active or planned improvements

---

## How Sheero Uses This Folder

Sheero references this when you're making location decisions, planning projects around the home, or researching where to live. Useful for tracking the research phase of a move or build.
