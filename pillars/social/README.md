# pillars/social — Social Life and Relationships

This pillar covers your relationships and social world — family, friends, romantic relationships, professional connections, and community involvement.

---

## What Belongs Here

- Relationship notes (important people, context, how to stay in touch)
- Social goals or intentions
- Outreach trackers (who you want to reconnect with, follow up with)
- Notes on social patterns you're working on

---

## Example Files

- `key_relationships.md` — people who matter and relevant context
- `outreach_tracker.md` — who you owe a message, when you last connected
- `social_goals.md` — what kind of social life you're building toward

---

## How RIGGS Uses This Folder

RIGGS can flag relationship maintenance tasks during check-ins, help you draft messages, and surface context about someone before an important conversation. Keep notes honest — this folder is for your benefit, not theirs.
