# pillars/physical — Physical Health

This pillar covers everything related to your physical body — fitness, nutrition, sleep, medical, and recovery.

---

## What Belongs Here

- Fitness plans and workout logs
- Nutrition guidelines or meal frameworks
- Medical notes, prescriptions, appointment history
- Sleep tracking or notes
- Recovery protocols (injury, illness)

---

## Example Files

- `fitness_plan.md` — current training approach and schedule
- `nutrition.md` — dietary guidelines, goals, or restrictions
- `medical_notes.md` — conditions, medications, provider contacts

---

## How Sheero Uses This Folder

Sheero reads this folder when you ask about physical health, fitness planning, or when foundational physical tasks are part of your daily routine. It does not proactively monitor or track — you bring it in as needed.
