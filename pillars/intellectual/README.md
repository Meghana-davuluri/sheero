# pillars/intellectual — Learning and Knowledge

This pillar covers everything related to your intellectual development — what you're learning, what you're reading, skills you're building, and knowledge you're accumulating.

---

## What Belongs Here

- Active learning paths and study plans
- Reading lists (books, articles, courses)
- Notes from courses, certifications, or deep dives
- Skill inventories — what you know, what you're building
- Research on topics you're actively studying

---

## Example Files

- `learning_plan.md` — current focus areas and study schedule
- `reading_list.md` — books and resources, organized by topic
- `notes/` — subfolder for notes from specific books or courses
- `skills.md` — skill inventory by domain

---

## How Sheero Uses This Folder

Sheero references this when you ask about learning priorities, skill gaps, or what to study next. It can help you build a study plan, suggest what to read given your goals, or surface notes from something you studied previously.
