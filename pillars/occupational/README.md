# pillars/occupational — Career and Income

This pillar covers everything related to how you earn a living — your career path, active job search, businesses, freelance or consulting work, and professional development.

---

## What Belongs Here

- Career profile and resume materials
- Job search plans and trackers
- Business plans and operations docs
- Client or consulting project notes
- Income stream tracking
- Professional development goals

---

## Example Files

- `career_profile.md` — full skills, experience, and positioning
- `resume.md` — current resume or resume variants
- `job_search/` — subfolder with search plan, board notes, application tracker
- `consulting/` — client work, proposals, project notes
- `income_streams.md` — all active and planned income sources

---

## How RIGGS Uses This Folder

RIGGS reads this when you ask about career direction, job search execution, business decisions, or income planning. It can help draft cover letters, evaluate opportunities, and track application progress.
